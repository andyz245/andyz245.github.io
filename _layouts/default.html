Andy Zhou

I am a graduate student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign advised by Bo Li. I also work closely with Yuxiong Wang and Haohan Wang.

My research interests are in LM agents and AI security. My work focuses on improving the capabilities and reliability of large language models, particularly for humanlike thinking domains such as reasoning, planning and autonomous decision-making.

I am also the founder and Head of Research at Lapis Labs, a student-led research group.
