About Me

I'm Andy Zhou and I'm the co-founder of Intology, the designers of Zochi, the world's first Artificial Scientist with peer-reviewed publications.

I was previously the co-founder and principal investigator at Lapis Labs, a student-led research group. We published more than 20 papers in top AI conferences and collaborated with many groups, including DeepMind, the Center for AI Safety, and IBM Research.

Email  /  GitHub  /  Google Scholar  /  Twitter

Research

My research focuses on advancing artificial intelligence with an emphasis on:

  • AI agents and autonomous systems
  • AI safety and alignment
  • Scientific discovery through AI

I'm particularly interested in developing AI systems that can reason, learn, and discover in ways that augment human capabilities and advance scientific knowledge. Check out my papers here.
